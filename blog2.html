The Importance and Power of YOUR Voice

By Nivega Kuhanesan

Scroll Down

Your voice is a powerful tool; it is the most powerful tool.

It doesn’t hurt to use your voice. You not using your voice does hurt, though.

We have access to technology, social media, sharing things online and voicing ourselves, but we need to realise that this has potential to help catalyse big changes for the better.

LINK



It’s time we start using our brains, pens, technology, voices and solidarity to manipulate the system. If you’ve been told otherwise, that this will not work, it’s because the people in power who benefit from exploitation of POC and deep rooted, subtle in some cases, racism within society fear the change that will arise once we use what we have in our grasps to our advantage.

I need you to have hope.

Because with solidarity and the drive and want for a change, a better future, a world where innocent people do not need to fear for their lives or fear every action they take, is possible. We all know that.

I need you to have hope.
I need you to use your voice.
Numbers, truth, justice, and solidarity scare those who benefit from the corruptness of society.

Together, we are powerful.
Let’s make change.
