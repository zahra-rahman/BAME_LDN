Welcome

BAME LDN is an informative blog dedicated to giving a platform to a community of different voices. We are students who understand that we are in the priviledged position where simple acts can lead to large impacts on society and we want to actively use our power to make change.

In order to amplify the view of others we have created a blog where many have expressed themselves through writing facsinating peices on topics they feel passionate about
