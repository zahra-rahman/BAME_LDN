“Resistance doesn’t have to be spectacular.

Most people assume that if they want to do something to change the world, they have to be Dr Martin Luther King or somebody like that, but what really matters is the development to the kind of consciousness that encourages us in many ways, in the smallest ways to resist and speak back to power” - Angela Davis. (CIIS Programme: Revisiting Angela Davis: A Life of Activism)


1 in 5 people cannot read or write. You can. How will you use this?

Many of us have been sheltered from the horrors of this world

But we need to open our eyes. Together, using our positions, education and platforms, we can catalyse in the healing of humanity.
Voices will be raised, and together, we can make voices be heard.

This website will be a platform for your voices, because it’s time we start working together. Everything counts.
You’ll find yourself with a lot of information on many issues you may not have heard about. Addressing these issues starts with acknowledgement, self-education, listening to voices and taking action, whether that be big or small.
With this platform, we hope to help you in becoming activists to whichever extent you are able to and to help you in doing your part in making this world a better place.
It’s time to open our eyes and listen to each other.
The future that is impossible now can become a possibility in the future. We must work together to make this a possibility for future generations.
The most powerful tools are: solidarity, knowledge and hope. Let’s use our position.
Love.
